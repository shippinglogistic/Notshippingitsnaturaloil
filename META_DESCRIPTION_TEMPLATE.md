## Common Mistakes to Avoid ❌

1. **Keyword Stuffing**
   - ❌ Bad: "Buy RSO RSO RSO cannabis oil RSO online RSO"
   - ✅ Good: "Buy premium RSO (Rick Simpson Oil) cannabis extract online. Lab-tested quality."

2. **Too Generic**
   - ❌ Bad: "Cannabis oil products available for purchase"
   - ✅ Good: "Lab-tested premium RSO and FECO with 72-95% THC. Discreet nationwide shipping."

3. **Too Long**
   - ❌ Bad: "This is a very long description that goes on and on and on explaining every detail about our products in excessive detail" (140+ chars, unclear)
   - ✅ Good: "Premium lab-tested cannabis oil. RSO and FECO available. Discreet shipping." (75 chars, clear)

4. **No CTA**
   - ❌ Bad: "RSO is a cannabis oil product"
   - ✅ Good: "Buy premium RSO online. Lab-tested, discreet shipping."

5. **Duplicate Descriptions**
   - ❌ Bad: Same description for all product pages
   - ✅ Good: Unique description for each product highlighting specific benefits

## SEO Tips for Meta Descriptions

### Do's ✅
1. **Include Primary Keyword** - Naturally within first 50 chars
2. **Be Specific** - Tell readers exactly what they'll find
3. **Highlight Benefits** - Why should they click?
4. **Include CTA** (optional) - "Learn more", "Shop now", "Discover"
5. **Make it Unique** - Don't duplicate across pages
6. **Match Page Content** - Description should match what's on the page

### Don'ts ❌
1. **Don't exceed 160 characters** - Gets cut off in search results
2. **Don't keyword stuff** - Looks spammy, hurts ranking
3. **Don't be vague** - Specific beats generic
4. **Don't use duplicates** - Each page needs unique meta
5. **Don't mislead** - Description must match content
6. **Don't use exact title match** - Vary from your H1/title
