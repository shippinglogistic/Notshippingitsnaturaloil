# Internal Linking Strategy & Best Practices

## Overview
Internal linking improves SEO by establishing information hierarchy, distributing page authority, and helping users navigate your site. These guidelines ensure consistent, strategic linking throughout naturalcannabisoil.shop.

## Primary Link Categories

### 1. Navigation Links (Header & Footer)
**Purpose:** Allow users to discover main content  
**Best Practices:**
- Keep header navigation focused on primary pages
- Use footer for comprehensive site map

### 5. Related Content Links
**Purpose:** Keep users engaged by suggesting related topics

#### "Related Products" Section
- Show 3-4 complementary products
- Use descriptive anchor text
- Place near bottom of product pages

#### "Related Articles" Section
- Show 3 recent or related blog posts
- Include brief description
- Place at end of blog articles

#### "You May Also Like" Section
- Show 2-3 related guides or resources
- Make visually distinct
- Use CTA language ("Explore more...")

## Anchor Text Guidelines

### Do's ✅
- Use descriptive, keyword-relevant text
- Make anchor text clear about link destination
- Use variations of target keywords
- Keep anchor text concise (2-5 words)

### Don'ts ❌
- Don't use "click here" or generic phrases
- Don't over-optimize with exact-match keywords
- Don't use different anchor text for same destination too frequently
- Don't link to irrelevant pages

### Examples

**Bad Anchor Text:**
- "click here"
- "read more"
- "link"
- "page"

**Good Anchor Text:**
- "RSO dosage guide" → /rso-dosage-guide
- "premium FECO products" → /products/feco
- "lab-tested cannabis oil" → /about or /certificates-of-analysis
- "RSO vs FECO comparison" → /rso-vs-feco
